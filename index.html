About

Learn more about me

Hello, I'm Adelina

Currently a student at the Faculty of Mathematics and Informatics at Sofia University with a great desire to develop in the field of UX / UI. I have been interested in information technology since I was a child. In high school, I participated in various hackathons, competitions related to programming, and extracurricular web programming courses. I organized and volunteered in events related to motivating and helping more women to learn about the possibilities of the IT field. During the first 3 years of my studies, I actively participated in the organization of various university initiatives as a member of the faculty student council. During my internship as a Back-End developer, I learned a lot about the processes in a company, teamwork, and software development.

  • Birthday: 15 January 1999
  • Located: Sofia, Bulgaria
  • Email: adelinatodorovaa@gmail.com
  • Remote: Available

In the first semester of my third academic year, I had the opportunity to participate in the course "Management of innovative software projects" at my faculty, and from there I became very passionate about UX and UI. I used to put = between UX and UI and I thought UX was entirely art. Personally, what interests me the most is the research of what people want, their needs, and their understandings because I have always had an interest in psychology. In the fall of 2020, Sofia University gave us the opportunity to take courses at Coursera for free, I personally did not know where to start, but I chose a course and so I took "Understanding User Needs" at the University of Michigan, where the use of interviews was mainly studied. From there my curiosity increased and now I want to develop a career as UX/UI designer.
